## ROLLUP 함수
- 일반적인 GROUP BY 절 사용
```sql
SELECT B.DNAME, A.JOB, COUNT(*) AS EMP_CNT, SUM(A.SAL) AS SAL_SUM
    FROM EMP A, DEPT B
    WHERE B.DEPTNO=A.DEPTNO
    GROUP BY B.DNAME=A.JOB;
```
